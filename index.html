We hope this letter finds you well. We are members of the SGM club studying in the 4th semester in the college. As you might be aware, the SGM club is a non-profit social welfare club of the ABV-IIITM college functioning since 2012. The club helps the children who live near the campus to get free and quality education. The alumni of this college started this club which is driven by the goal of spreading knowledge and facilitating a valuable learning experience for those in need.

In continuing the trailblazing service path charted by our club seniors and in the relentless pursuit of achieving our motto, we are organizing the “Annual SGM Science Fair”. Our Science Fair aims to provide an environment for budding scientists to showcase their projects, experiments, and innovations. It is an opportunity for students to exchange ideas, learn from each other, and contribute to the advancement of their scientific knowledge.

We believe that your expertise and experience would be a huge asset to our event. We are confident that your insights will encourage the students and foster a spirit of passion for science and technology.
